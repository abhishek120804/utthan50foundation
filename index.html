Education Program (Our Work)

We work to provide education support to underprivileged children by supplying learning resources, guidance, and motivation. Our mission is to create a better future through knowledge.

Food Distribution

Utthan50foundation actively organizes food distribution drives to ensure that no one sleeps hungry. We believe food is a basic right for everyone.

Clothes Distribution

We distribute clothes to those in need, especially during winters and emergencies, helping them live with dignity and warmth.

About Us

Utthan50foundation is a social initiative working towards uplifting society through education, food distribution, and clothing support. We believe that education is the strongest path to empowerment.
